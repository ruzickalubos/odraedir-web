Biography

Odraedir is a Prague-based pagan metal band formed in the fall of 2009. Although it operates with a traditional setup of 2 guitars, bass, drums, and vocals, the music is spiced up with various folk instruments such as violins, bagpipes, and flutes.

Odraedir is known for their presence on the scene across the entire country. In October 2023, the band released their second full-length album titled "Vengeance," and during 2024, they presented songs from the new album at club events and festivals in the Czech Republic and abroad. The album emphasizes quality production, which highlights the composition and arrangement of each song, and also captivates with a powerful combination of epic choruses and melodic death metal riffs enriched with some unconventional elements of pagan metal. The stories then draw listeners into Norse mythology, and some of them are brought closer to the audience through lyrics videos. This year, Odraedir released two new videos to songs from the upcoming EP Abyss. The songs Cult and Sip of Courage are a reminiscence of former music and particularly, Cult is the very first song composed in the workshop of Odraedir, now presented in a novel and more powerful robe.

Legal Information

Odraedir, z.s. is a registered association listed in the Commercial Register under ID No. 23566141, established on August 21, 2025.

Scope of Activities:

  • Performing in the Czech Republic and abroad
  • Promoting alternative music culture and Norse mythology
  • Collaborating with domestic and international music groups and partners
  • Organizing cultural and music events, including accompanying programs (e.g. lectures, workshops, thematic activities)
  • Selling music media and promotional items (merchandise)
  • Buying and selling themed music or historically inspired products (e.g. pendants, posters)
